IMAGE Data Portal Innovative Management of Animal Genetic Resources

"Innovative Management of Animal Genetic Resources" (acronym IMAGE) is a project funded by the Horizon 2020 Research and Innovation Programme of the European Union.

IMAGE strategy

The aim of IMAGE is to enhance the use of genetic collections and to upgrade animal gene bank management. IMAGE will further develop genomic methodologies, biotechnologies, and bioinformatics for enhanced knowledge and exploitation of animal genetic resources.

Enhancing the Use of Gene Bank Data

The IMAGE data system provides

  • Integration
  • Minimising genetic accidents such as abnormalities or genetic variability tipping points;
  • Standardization
  • Sustainability
  • Visibility
  • Ease of uploading
  • Integration with existing databases
The IMAGE web portal One of the main goals of the project was the creation of a European web portal that integrates data from gene banks and collections with genomics data, geographical information system data, and other information generated by IMAGE
